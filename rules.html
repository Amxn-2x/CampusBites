Ordering Rules

Simple guidelines to keep orders fast, fair, and hassle-free.

01

Orders Are Final

Once an order is placed, it cannot be cancelled or modified. Please review items carefully before confirming.

02

Single Order at a Time

Only one active order per customer is allowed. This helps us manage campus rush efficiently.

03

Accurate Details Required

Make sure your name and contact details are correct. Incorrect info may delay or cancel your order.

04

Pickup Responsibility

Orders must be collected within the given time window. Unclaimed orders won’t be refunded.

Payment Rules

Transparent payments. No confusion. No last-minute surprises.

01

Prepaid Orders Only

All orders must be paid in advance. Cash on delivery is not available for campus orders.

02

No Refund Policy

Payments once made are non-refundable. This includes order cancellations or no-shows.

03

Correct Amount Required

Ensure the total amount is paid correctly. Partial or incorrect payments may lead to order rejection.

04

Payment Confirmation

Orders are processed only after successful payment confirmation. Please wait for approval before pickup.

Pickup & Hygiene Rules

Clean food. Organized pickup. Respect for everyone’s time.

01

Pickup on Time

Please collect your order within the given pickup window. Delayed pickups may affect food quality.

02

Show Order Confirmation

Carry your order confirmation while picking up. This helps us serve faster and avoid confusion.

03

No Crowd at Counter

Maintain distance and wait for your turn. This ensures smooth flow and hygienic handling.

04

Hygiene Is Mandatory

Our staff follows strict hygiene standards. We expect customers to cooperate and keep the area clean.

Feedback or Facing an Issue?

We believe good systems improve through honest feedback. If something didn’t feel right — food, pickup, or experience — let us know directly.
